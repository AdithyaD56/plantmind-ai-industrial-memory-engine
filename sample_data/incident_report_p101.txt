Incident Report - Pump P101 Failure Event
Date: 2025-09-03
Location: Unit A

Summary:
Pump P101 experienced an unplanned trip after sustained vibration, bearing wear, and oil contamination.

Root Cause:
Inadequate lubrication and delayed corrective maintenance.

Impact:
- 3.5 hours downtime
- Reduced throughput
- Maintenance work order raised for inspection and alignment

Lessons Learned:
- Similar vibration trend was seen in a prior event eight months earlier.
- Bearing inspection frequency should be increased.
